# 05 — Decision Trees

Grounded in the [RippedBody Training Pyramid](https://rippedbody.com/how-to-build-training-programs/)
(Muscle and Strength Pyramid: Training).

## Execution order

```
1. training_split     → frequency / split by days/week
2. weekly_volume      → sets per muscle group per week
3. intensity_scheme   → reps and RIR by goal + experience
4. exercise_selection → include/exclude/substitute by environment
5. session_density    → work:rest ratios by goal + session length
6. progression_rule   → progression scheme by experience
7. periodisation      → periodisation scheme + deload frequency
```

## Training splits (by days/week)

| Days | Split | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| 2 | Full Body A/B | Beginners, time-constrained |
| 3 | Full Body A/B/C | Most people (RippedBody default) |
| 4 | Upper/Lower (A/B) | Intermediate hypertrophy |
| 5 | Push/Pull/Legs + Push/Pull | Advanced |
| 6 | PPL × 2 | Advanced with high recovery capacity |

## Volume (sets per muscle group per week)

| Goal | Beginner | Intermediate | Advanced |
|---|---|---|---|
| Strength | 8 | 12 | 16 |
| Fat loss | 8 | 10 | 12 |
| Muscle gain / recomp | 10 | 14 | 18 |

Practical range: 10-20 sets/muscle/week (RippedBody Pyramid).

## Intensity (RIR)

| Rep range | RIR guideline |
|---|---|
| 4-6 reps | 4-0 RIR |
| 6-8 reps | 3-0 RIR |
| 8-12 reps | 2-0 RIR |
| >12 reps | 1-0 RIR |

Beginners stay ≥3 RIR; advanced push closer to failure.

## Progression by experience

| Experience | Scheme | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Beginner | Linear | Add load/reps each session |
| Intermediate | Double progression | Hit top of rep range, then add 2.5 kg |
| Advanced | Periodised / auto-regulated | Wave loading, RPE caps, deload every 4 weeks |

## Rest periods

| Goal | Rest |
|---|---|
| Strength | 3-5 min |
| Hypertrophy | 1.5-3 min |
| Fat loss | 60-90s |

Express sessions (30 min) use supersets/circuits to compress time.
